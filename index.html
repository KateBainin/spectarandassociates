Firm Overview

Spector and Associates is a pan-African business law firm with independent offices in five (5) countries and a network of firms in 30 African countries. At Spector & Associates, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional legal services with integrity, professionalism, and expertise. Our firm provides comprehensive legal solutions across various practice areas, including corporate law, real estate, family law, immigration, and litigation. With a team of experienced attorneys, we are committed to understanding our clients' unique needs and offering strategic legal counsel tailored to their objectives. Whether you are an individual, a business, or a multinational corporation, we prioritize your interests and work tirelessly to achieve favorable outcomes.

Our Services

At Spector & Associates, we provide expert legal counsel across a wide range of practice areas. Our Work spans across sectors such as family law, corporate matters, real estate, immigration or litigation. Our experienced attorneys are committed to delivering strategic solutions tailored to your needs.

Immigration law

We ensure that our clients are in compliance with both international and local labour laws and local content regulations of the country.

Family, Trust & Probate

Our dedicated family law attorneys are here to provide compassionate guidance and strong legal representation to protect your rights and the well-being of your loved ones.

Real Estate and Construction

Whether you’re a homebuyer, developer, contractor, or investor, our experienced attorneys are here to help you navigate complex real estate and construction laws with confidence.

Natural Resources

Our firm provides expert legal guidance in natural resource law, ensuring compliance, sustainability, and responsible management of land, water, minerals, and energy resources.

Corporate Law

We provide comprehensive corporate law services to help businesses navigate complex legal landscapes. Be it a startup, a growing enterprise, or an established corporation, our experienced attorneys offer strategic guidance on entity formation, mergers and acquisitions, among others.

Intellectual property law

We provide tailored legal strategies to safeguard your intellectual property rights. Whether you’re an entrepreneur, artist, or corporation, we are committed to protecting what makes your work unique.
